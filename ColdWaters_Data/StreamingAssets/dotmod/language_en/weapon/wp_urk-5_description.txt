WeaponDescription=Special: Dual-purpose\nThe URK-5 "Rastrub-B" (NATO classification: SS-N-14 Silex) is a modernization of the URPK-3 "Metel". Anti-ship mode was introduced and it could be used both against surface and submerged targets, giving ships like the Krivak ASuW capabilities without reducing storage for their ASW missiles. Mode can be switched in the weapon settings just before launch.
