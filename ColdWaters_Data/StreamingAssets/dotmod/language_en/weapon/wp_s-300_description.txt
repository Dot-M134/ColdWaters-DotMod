WeaponDescription=Special: Universal\n\nS-300F "Fort" SAM (NATO reporting name: SA-N-6 Grumble) is a Soviet naval long range surface-to-air missile system. Guidance system is universal and allows for switching between missile, aircraft and ship-attack mode. Mode can be switched in the weapon settings just before launch.
