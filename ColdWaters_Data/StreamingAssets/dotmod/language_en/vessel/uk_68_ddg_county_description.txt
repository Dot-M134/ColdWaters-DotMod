History=The County class was a class of guided missile destroyer, the first such vessels built by the Royal Navy. Designed specifically around the Sea Slug anti-aircraft missile system, the primary role of these ships was area air-defense around the aircraft carrier task force in the nuclear-war environment.\nAs constructed, the County-class ships were armed with a pair of twin QF 4.5-inch gun mountings.
