AircraftDescriptiveName=Ilyushin IL-38 May
History=Most aptly described as the Soviet equivalent of the US Lockheed P-3, the IL-38 is a medium-range antisubmarine aircraft, derived from the IL-18 turboprop airliner. It is capable of laying huge barriers of sonobuoys, and then pressing home the attack with an assortment of depth charges and homing torpedoes.
